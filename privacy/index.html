Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1 Introduction

BabyBite ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use BabyBite.

By using the App, you consent to the practices described here.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Baby Profile: Name, date of birth, gender, feeding type, dietary preferences, allergies, profile photos
  • Growth Data: Weight and height measurements you enter
  • Preferences: Language, units, region, notification settings
  • Milestones: Development milestones you track

2.2 Automatically Collected

  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • App usage data to improve experience
  • Anonymous crash reports

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

🛡️ We do not collect parent/caregiver real names, location data, financial information, or sell any data to third parties.

3 How We Use Information

  • Generate personalized meal plans based on baby's age and preferences
  • Track growth and developmental milestones
  • Provide allergy-aware food suggestions
  • Send helpful feeding reminders (with your permission)
  • Improve app features and user experience

4 Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

📱 All baby profile data, growth entries, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your baby's personal information to our servers.

4.2 Subscription Data

Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat with an anonymous user identifier — no personal child data is shared.

4.3 Security

We use industry-standard security practices, but no electronic storage method is 100% secure.

5 Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (anonymous ID only)
  • Google Play / App Store: Payment processing and distribution
  • Expo: App updates and push notifications

Each has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

6 Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

🛡️ BabyBite is designed for parents and caregivers of babies (ages 0–3). While the app's content is directed at children, it is operated exclusively by parents/guardians.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

All baby profile data (name, date of birth, allergies, etc.) is entered by the parent/caregiver and stored locally on the device only. No child data is transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) are disabled in compliance with COPPA.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us at devmile45@gmail.com for immediate deletion.

7 Notifications

With your permission, we send notifications for:

  • Daily meal reminders
  • Weekly prep shopping reminders
  • New food introduction suggestions
  • Developmental milestone reminders

Disable anytime via App or device settings.

8 Your Rights

  • Access: View all data in the App anytime
  • Delete: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings or uninstall
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and analytics anytime
  • Portability: Data stays locally on your device

9 Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

10 Data Deletion Request

🌐 Google Play Requirement: Although all personal data is stored locally on your device, you may request the deletion of any auxiliary data (such as anonymous subscription identifiers) by contacting us.

To request data deletion, please email devmile45@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 48 hours.
